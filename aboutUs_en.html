About Us

Stoner’s Den was created to bring high quality CBD products to your neighborhood. We provide a wide variety of different variants of the CBD plant and we test all of our products ourselves to ensure the level of quality. These products include creams and physiotherapy products, as well as teas and gummies for your preferred methods of use, including flowers for the old school way.

We are located in the district of Sarria-Sant Gervasi, right around the corner of Turo park. Any new user of CBD can be offered help at the counter or through email to match the best products for your needs. We also offer online sales on our website to allow you access to your treatment all over Europe.
